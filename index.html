Newry & MourneQuest

Welcome to Newry & MourneQuest.

Allow me to introduce myself - I am Cosabian Brohan Hawksbeard, eighth son of Poitin Hawksbeard, The Great Hunter, Maker of Dreams, Third Lord of Caiserban and Bringer of the Nightfall Rainbow.

But you can call me "Cobs".

I am a Clurichaun, a dandelion hunter, and I was born on All Hallow's Eve, on the night of a full moon in the year 1411.

Normally I dwell in a mansion within the Curraghard Tree in the True Kingdom of Mourne and the most beautiful of all the realms. It stretches from Annalong Wood to the Kissing Gate and from the Forests of Tollymore to the shores of Rostrevor where the rivers run clear and the hills are forever green.

I have come to your realm for I am in dire need of your help. The dreaded Shimnavore has captured my dearest friend Jack Turner. Jack is eleven years old and he is human like you, but he was born in 1836 in Springwell Port. He is the second son of Martha and Matthew Turner and he has in his possession a most powerful secret - a secret the terrible Shimnavore will stop at nothing to obtain. Somehow, Jack and the Shimnavore have travelled through time and with some difficulty, I have been able to follow them all the way here to the City of the Yew - which you call Newry. But the trail has grown cold and I need the eyes of a fresh young mind to help me in my Quest.

Because I know Jack Turner well, I am sure he has left a clues to his whereabouts all over your wonderful city. I have a map but I am unsure where it leads. Will you join me on a journey and unlock the hidden secrets that Newry holds?
